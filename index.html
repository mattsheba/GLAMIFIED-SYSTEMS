Frequently asked questions

Does GlamifiedHR work without internet? ▾
Yes. GlamifiedHR is a desktop application that runs entirely on your Windows PC. You only need internet to activate your license and download updates.
How current are the tax rates? ▾
GlamifiedHR uses current ZRA PAYE bands, NAPSA contribution rates (5% + 5%), and NHIMA rates (1% + 1%). When ZRA announces changes, we release a free update.
Can I use it on more than one computer? ▾
Each license allows activation on up to 2 machines. If you need more, contact us for a discounted additional license.
What happens after I buy a license key? ▾
Your license key is emailed immediately after payment. Download the GlamifiedHR installer from this site, install it on your Windows PC, enter your key when prompted, and you're running payroll within the hour.
Can I import my existing employee data? ▾
Yes. GlamifiedHR supports CSV import for employee data. Prepare a spreadsheet with your employee details and import them all at once during setup.
Is my data safe? ▾
Your data is stored locally on your computer in an encrypted database. We never have access to your data. The software includes backup and restore features.
