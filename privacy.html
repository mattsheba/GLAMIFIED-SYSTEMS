Last updated: May 2026

Glamified Systems ("we", "us", or "our") operates glamifiedsystems.com and its associated products: GlamifiedHR, CVPro Zambia, and Glamified Jobs. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly:

  • Contact form submissions: name, email, phone number, company name, and message
  • Purchase information: name, email, phone number (used to process payment via Lenco)
  • Job applications: CV details submitted through Glamified Jobs or CVPro Zambia
  • Employer accounts: company name, contact details, job posting content

GlamifiedHR is a desktop application: your employee and payroll data is stored locally on your computer. We do not have access to your GlamifiedHR data.

2. How we use your information

  • To process purchases and deliver license keys by email
  • To respond to enquiries and provide support
  • To send service-related communications (e.g. license delivery, update notices)
  • To operate the Glamified Jobs platform and match job seekers with employers

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Third-party services

We use the following third-party services to operate our platform:

  • Lenco: payment processing. Your payment details are handled by Lenco and subject to their privacy policy.
  • Supabase: database and authentication for CVPro Zambia and Glamified Jobs.
  • Google Fonts: font loading (no personal data collected).

4. Data retention

We retain contact and enquiry records for up to 2 years. Purchase records are retained for 5 years for accounting purposes. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

5. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. To make a request, contact us at info.glamifiedsystems@gmail.com or via WhatsApp at +260 977 669 883.

6. Cookies
