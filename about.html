Our story

Built by Zambians,
for Zambian business

Technology in Zambia has always been imported — built elsewhere, adapted reluctantly, and never quite right. We started Glamified Systems to change that. HR software, career platforms, IT consulting: everything we build is designed from scratch, for how Zambia actually works.

Our mission

To become Zambia's default HR technology platform

Every Zambian business — from a 5-person clinic to a 500-employee manufacturer — should have access to proper HR systems, statutory compliance tools, and talent management. Not imported software that doesn't understand Zambia. Ours.

Our journey

How we got here

2022
Glamified Systems founded in Lusaka. Business consulting services begin. First SME clients.
2025
CVPro Zambia launched — Zambia's first dedicated CV builder. Hundreds of job seekers sign up.
2025
GlamifiedHR development begins. 12 months of research into Zambian payroll and HR requirements.
2026
GlamifiedHR beta launched with pilot clients. Payroll, NAPSA, NHIMA, and PAYE fully implemented.
2026
Full public launch. Glamified Jobs launched. Target: 50 paying clients by end of year.
2027 →
GlamifiedHR SaaS cloud version. Employee self-service portal. Mobile app. National scale.
